Stanchion

Dynamic Crowd Control

what we do

Stanchion delivers dynamic crowd control with predictive analysis. Stanchions of the past were static, unable to adjust for changing traffic flow without manual intervention. Stanchion enables simple and automatic adjustment via image recognition and mechanization.

our clients

Anywhere you can find a long line, you can find a use for Stanchion. Stanchion can facilitate crowd control at airports, amusement parks, movie theaters, and large-scale events like concerts and hackathons.For these clients, Stanchion can serve as a cost-saving mechanism, avoiding wages wasted on manual adjustments while providing a better experience for attendees. Additionally, in high security settings, Stanchion's image recognition capabilities can aid entities like TSA in securing points of entry and avoiding security breaches.
